Winter School

So you’ve heard all about data journalism and data-driven storytelling and you’d like to learn more...

What's this about?

The Winter School aims to bridge that gap by training working journalists in the fundamentals of data journalism. We will also offer training in social media as a journalism tool, with a focus on discovery and verification of content.
Participants will be introduced to all aspects of data and social media journalism, with structured course material. This will be coupled with hands-on practical work to ensure that you are able to translate what you’ve learned into great storytelling.
The aim is for participants to “hit the ground running” and be able to be able to incorporate their new skills in their day-to-day works as journalists.
To lay to rest one misconception: you don’t need to be a coder or have technical skills to practice data journalism.
What you need is a nose for a good story to help you explore the data and find the stories hidden in the data.
Bottom line: it’s called data JOURNALISM, not DATA journalism.

When?

Late June/early July consisting of an intensive two day boot camp, followed by three morning sessions over the next three weeks.

What do we cover?

Data Proficiency

Basic gathering, collating and verification of data.

Data Viz and Storytelling

Uncover the stories hidden in the data and how best to tell and visualise your story.

The Journalist's Toolkit

Free tools that will help you to be a better journalist.

Social Media Journalism

How to use social media as a journalism tool, with a focus on verification of social media content and free tools.

Who should apply?

Freelance and full-time journalists interested in adding data and social media journalism to their skill set.

What is provided?

  • Equipment
    • Ideally you should have your own laptop, but computers will be provided for those who do not have one.
  • Lunch and refreshments
  • Travel assistance
    • We are based in Claremont, close to train stations and taxi routes. We understand travelling can be a prohibitive expense for some candidates and we will provide transport assistance for those able to motivate it.
