Data Journalism Cadet School

It was found that media management did not always see the value of data journalism and how it could be monetised. They were not prepared to invest in growing skills and resources. In addition, as few journalism schools are offering journalism in their curricula, a dearth of skills exist in South Africa. The result is that existing practitioners are often scarce and prohibitively expensive.

The Winter School aims to fill this gap, teaching cadets the value of data and data-driven journalism. Over the course of xxx weeks, cadets will be introduced to all aspects of data journalism and will be led through structured course material, coupled with hands-on practical experience, to ensure the learnings are fully internalised.

What do we cover?

Basic Journalism

Brush up on some of the basics, including...

Data Proficiency

Learn how to discover data sources, scrape and clean the data.

Data Vis and Storytelling

Uncover the story your data tells you and learn best how to visually represent your findings.

Social Media Journalism

Learn how to discover social media data and how to use social media as a verification tool.

The Journalist's Toolkit

Master open source data and social media tools.

Who should apply?

Student candidates will be selected on the basis of previous practical journalism training and experience and a desire to improve the quality of South African journalism. Whilst practical experience is preferred, this can also include practical experience gained as part of tertiary education. Students to have demonstrated interest beyond lectures.

What is provided?

  • Equipment
    • Students are encouraged to bring their own laptops if they have them. This will allow us to assist in setting the machines up with everything they need to hit the ground running.Alternatively, computers will be available for those who need.
  • Lunch and refreshments
    • To ensure students can focus on the learning at hand, we will supply a healthy, nutritious lunch. Our kitchen, stocked with all sorts of coffees and teas, is available to all cadets during the course of the winter school.
  • Travel assistance
    • We understand travelling can be a prohibitive expense for many of our candidates and we offer transport assistance, based on merit of application, to ease this burden somewhat.
